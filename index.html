RESOLVING YOUR INQUIRIES

Everything you need to know about planning your stay at the Chulpakanqi estate.

The estate features 4 independent bungalows. Each bungalow can accommodate families comfortably with its 3-bedroom layout, custom bunk bedding, and full living amenities. The entire property can be reserved exclusively for up to 24 guests, making it ideal for family gatherings and corporate retreats.

Each luxury cabin booking includes access to all common amenities (infinity pool, stone grill area, football field). A bespoke private chef service, preparing traditional Andean and contemporary cuisine in your bungalow, can be arranged at an additional customized package rate. Contact our concierge team to coordinate your menu preferences.

Chulpakanqi is located in an exclusive, secure pocket of the Sacred Valley hills, exactly a 10-minute private drive from the historical Plaza de Armas of Cusco. Cusco's Alejandro Velasco Astete International Airport is approximately a 15-minute drive from the property. We offer private luxury transfers for all arrivals and departures.

Safety is a cornerstone of the Chulpakanqi experience. The estate is a fully gated compound with 24/7 dedicated professional security staff, controlled private access gates, and absolute perimeter privacy, ensuring a completely secure environment for high-profile executives and families.
